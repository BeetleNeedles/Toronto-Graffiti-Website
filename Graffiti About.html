About

"Toronto Graffiti" is a student project site dedicated to archiving the graffiti found across the city of Toronto. When looking closely, you find that graffiti in the city is as common as you think it is, we are simply desensitized to the presence of so we forget that it’s a part of our daily lives.

Graffiti is seen as many things, art, vandalism, or a form of a statement, but it is a brief glimpse into what events took place when it was made. By archiving, we are able to look back to analyze how our world has adapted and reacted to societal changes, or admire what something once stood in digital form. All made by people of different races, ages, and perspectives.
